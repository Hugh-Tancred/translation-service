Legal expertise

Terminology that lawyers recognise

Our translation handles the vocabulary of European legal documents as a specialist would — not as a generalist AI processing text in isolation.

Statutory concepts

Tone and register

Formal European legal prose into formal English legal prose. We replicate the secondary qualities of the source text.
