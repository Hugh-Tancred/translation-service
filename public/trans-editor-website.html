What you should know

  • This service translates into English only. We do not currently offer translation into other languages.
  • We accept documents in European languages. Documents in non-European languages cannot currently be processed.
  • We translate one document at a time. Batch submissions are not supported.
  • This is a fully automated service. No human reviews your document or checks the translation.
  • AI translation is very good, but not infallible. We recommend treating this as a working translation rather than a certified one.
  • If your document is legally or financially consequential—a contract you'll sign, medical instructions you'll follow, evidence for court—we recommend also consulting a qualified human translator.
  • This service does not produce certified translations. If a certified or sworn translation is required, please consult a qualified human translator.
  • The translated document will be clearly laid out, but will not exactly replicate complex original formatting such as tables or multi-column layouts.
  • Delivery is usually within a few minutes but is not guaranteed. If you provided an email address, your document will be sent there on completion.
  • Your document is deleted from our servers within 48 hours of delivery.
